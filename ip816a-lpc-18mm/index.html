Overview

VIVOTEK’s IP816A-LPC (18mm) is a professional box network cameras offering perfect solutions for License Plate Capture with a unique headlight filter design and specialized firmware for traffic monitoring.

Featuring dedicated LPC firmware and mutually compatible lens, the IP816A-LPC (18mm) is perfect for parking related applications. With a suitable selection of external IR LEDs, this camera will be ideal for 24/7 license plate capture and will provide high-detail polished images for reliable license plate recognition.

Equipped with a P-iris lens for extreme precision images, a headlight filter design which blocks distracting lights, and Full HD sensor enabling viewing resolution of 1920x1080 at a smooth 30 fps, the IP816A-LPC (18mm) is capable of capturing high quality and high resolution video in any conditions. With up to 2 Megapixel with WDR Pro, 3DNR and SNV technology, no matter whether in high contrast or in low light environments, this camera is high-tech workhorses which will ensure license plate capture and traffic monitoring of the highest precision.

In order to help properly adjust the focus of this high resolution camera, the IP816A-LPC (18mm) is equipped with VIVOTEK’ s RBF (Remote Back Focus) System, allowing installers to adjust focus even more accurately in complicated or sensitive installation environments. Meanwhile, value-added features like EIS and Snapshot Focus ensure that the IP816A-LPC (18mm) performs as the perfect LPR solution by empowering installers to adjust camera positions and focus with extreme precision.
