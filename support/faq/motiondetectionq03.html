How to setup the motion windows for 8000 series cameras released after 7/1/2011

Introduction

With our newer releases of our cameras, there is a compatibility issue with Internet Explorer 8 and 9. When trying to create or modify motion detection windows in the cameras interface, the user may experience the inability to move or resize the motion detection window once it is created.

  1. Question
    1. When I click the corner of the motion detection window to resize, I click to drag but nothing happens. How can I move/resize the window?
  2. How to do/Step by Step
    1. Navigate to your motion detection settings in your VIVOTEK configuration settings.
    2. Click the 'TOOLS' menu bar option in Internet Explorer. (if you do not see a menu-bar, press the 'ALT' key once on your keyboard to reveal the menu bar) Then click on 'Compatibility View Settings' to open another dialogue box to add the cameras IP address in permanently so you will no longer need to go through these steps in the future with this specific camera/IP address. Another dialogue box will open when you click on 'compatibility view settings', click 'ADD' then 'CLOSE'. The interface should refresh and you will gain access to move/resize the motion window.
    3. You can also click on the compatibility-view button located next to the 'Refresh' and 'STOP' button to the right of the URL bar. You may need to do this each time you log into the camera.
