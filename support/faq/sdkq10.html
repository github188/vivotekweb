Configuring a network camera for use in Skype

  1. Home
  2. FAQ
  3. SDK Q10
  1. Download and install VIVOTEK Video Capture Filter
    1. Log in at the SDK Page, then download the .zip file called "Audio Video Capture Filter".
    2. After downloading the .zip file, install Video Capture Filter, then reboot your computer.
  2. Video Capture Filter Setting
    1. When you log in to your computer, you will see a dialog window like the one shown in the figure below. Enter the requested information for your camera.
    2. If you do not wish to save the entered settings, click Cancel.
  3. Start Skype
    1. Start Skype, then sign in to your account.
    2. Select Tools → Options ...
    3. Click Video settings → Select webcam.
    4. You can click Webcam Settings to access and change the Video Capture Filter settings.
    5. After everything is properly set, you will be able to start a video call.
