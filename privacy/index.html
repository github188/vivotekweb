Why VIVOCloud Service Accesses Your Device

VIVOCloud app will access the following components or information of your mobile devices as below:

  • Phone status and UID
    We collect phone status and UID to analyze usage status, and improve our services.
  • Camera
    Camera is used for QR code scan.
  • Microphone
    Microphone is used when you use 2-way audio in VIVOCloud app.
  • Location (approximate location based on Internet hot spot)
    We collect approximate location based on Internet address for data analysis and research to improve our service.
  • SD card
    Screenshot will be saved to your SD card.
  • Internet
    VIVOCloud service require internet connection.
  • Prevent mobile phone from hibernating
    When viewing video streaming, this function will keep screen on.

We collect and use the following information to provide, improve and protect our services:

    We collect information, which includes, but not limited to IP address, type of your mobile devices, identifiers associated with your devices, connection speed and connection time. Your devices may also transmit location information to the services.

    VIVOTEK device information including MAC, device ID, IP address, device model, number of channels, and number of device push notifications (not content) will be collected when connecting to VIVOCloud service.
