Overview

iViewer is developed to allow users to monitor locations remotely by providing access to surveillance camera video directly from iOS or Android handheld device.

The re-designed and easy-to-use interface allows users to monitor live video from hundreds of cameras within their fingers. The user may access cameras managed by VAST (central & video management software), VIVOTEK NVR, or make direct connections to individual cameras via IP address or LAN search. iViewer also allows access to two way audio for camera, PTZ and PiP screen control, fisheye dewarp, snapshot and the capability to playback recorded video from VIVOTEK VAST and NVR.

Multiple Layout

With 1x2, 2x3 and 3x4 layout, users can pick most fit layout based on various situation or purpose. Simply tap bottom-left layout icon to change most suitable layout.

Timeline Playback

Provide users intuitively timeline playback experience. Drag directly on the timeline to choose the playback time. The data indicator on the timeline can display whether there is recorded data or not during a certain period. Users can play recorded clips from VAST, VIVOTEK NVRs or VIVOTEK cameras with supported firmware.

Fisheye Dewarp

Fisheye Dewarp of VIVOTEK camera can also be done even in mobile app. Users can choose 1O, 1P, 1R mode of fisheye camera and use ePTZ to view stream from different angle.
