VIVOTEK to Debut FD816C-H, the World's Smallest Recessed-Mount Network Camera

VIVOTEK, with solid R&D resources and extensive experience in the field of surveillance, will announce the world's smallest 2-Megapixel recessed-mount fixed dome network camera, the FD816C-H. Sporting a diameter of only 60 mm, and featuring the advanced Wide Dynamic Range Technology, WDR Pro, the FD816C-H provides superior visibility in high-contrast lighting environments. 3D Noise Reduction and SNV (Supreme Night Visibility) technology further increase the performance of the camera and mean users will enjoy clear, polished video with full color under low-light conditions. With its low-profile and high-level features, the VIVOTEK FD816C-H provides an ideal solution for discreet indoor applications, such as banks, retail stores, restaurants, offices and more.

 

VIVOTEK to Launch World's First Adjustable Tilt Angle Fisheye Camera, the CC8370-HV

VIVOTEK will release a vandal-proof cube network camera, the CC8370-HV, offering up to 30 fps at 3-Megapixel resolution. Featuring 180° horizontal panoramic views with adjustable tilt angle, the camera allows users amazing flexibility of viewing angles and options, with zero blind spots. Further, WDR Pro, SNV (Supreme Night Visibility) and 3D Noise Reduction technology all combine to enable the CC8370-HV to capture clear and polished video in all lighting conditions. In addition, unique anti-ligature design, IP66- and IK10-rated housing reduce the risk of both self-harm, splash and vandalism, making the camera idea for a wide range of applications, such as boutique, department, convenience stores as well as schools, police stations and prisons.
