VIVOTEK to Launch the New Height-Strip Network Camera, CC8130(HS)

VIVOTEK will officially launch the new height-strip network camera, CC8130(HS), equipped with a 180 degree wide-angle lens, delivering clear images for reliable facial recognition. An ideal installation height of 170cm (5.5 ft) allows the CC8130(HS) to capture clear facial images at eye view, even when the subject is wearing a hat or hood. In addition, the plug and play design makes the CC8130(HS) installation simple and rapid. Combining low-profile and high-level features, the CC8130(HS) is ideal for discreet indoor applications, such as retail, banks, restaurants, small businesses and particularly in cashier areas.

 

VIVOTEK to Unveil IP816A-LPC, License Plate Capture Solution for Traffic Monitoring

Leveraging the superior quality of the its IP816A-HP, VIVOTEK will shortly release a dedicated solution for LPC (License Plate Capture) applications, the IP816A-LPC. Constituting a total solution for traffic monitoring, the IP816A-LPC features a 2MP LPC camera with corresponding CS-mount lens and IR LED. Compared to a conventional D1 camera, the IP816A-LPC can achieve wider coverage of up to 2 lanes and a longer range of up to 20M for plate recognition and 100M for road view.

The IP816A-LPC is also integrated with overseer LPR software, developed by VIT company, which is one of the famous LPR software manufacturers in the world, ensuring the IP816A-LPC camera to provide a captured license plate image for LPR applications.

 

VIVOTEK to Release Wide Range of Enclosures and IR Illuminators, Offering Comprehensive Solutions for all Customers

VIVOTEK, showing its ongoing commitment to becoming a total surveillance solution provider, will release a wide range of outdoor enclosures and IR illuminators, fully compatible with all VIVOTEK box network cameras. VIVOTEK will provide four new outdoor enclosures: the AE-236 and AE-237, equipped with defog, blower, 24 AC power input and fiber interface; and the AE-241 and AE-242 which feature built-in IR LED, defog, fan and UPoE power input, enabling single-cable installation. In addition to these enclosures, VIVOTEK will further offer two new IR illuminators: the AI-104 with a range of up to 100M, and the AI-105 with a 200M maximum range. Harmonizing network cameras, enclosures and IR illuminators, VIVOTEK’s new total solutions are ideal for diverse outdoor applications, such as traffic monitoring, city surveillance, and sports stadiums.

 

VIVOTEK to Announce its State-of-the-art 16-CH Standalone NVR, ND8422P

VIVOTEK will announce its state-of-the-art Embedded Plug & Play NVR, ND8422P, allowing even small-scale operations to enjoy the benefits of VIVOTEK’s advanced systems. The ND8422P, while accessible to a wide range of users, is an extremely very powerful NVR. The Linux embedded ND8422P is a 16 channel standalone NVR with 8-port 802.3af complaint PoE, allowing connection with PoE cameras for a maximum 80 W power supply. VIVOTEK believes that smart technology should still be easy to operate, so the ND8422P comes equipped with “One Button Setup,” which allows plug-&-play and auto-setup functionality, making this advanced unit surprisingly simple to install.
