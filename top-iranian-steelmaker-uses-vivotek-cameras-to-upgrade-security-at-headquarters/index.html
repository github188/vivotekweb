Top Iranian Steelmaker Uses VIVOTEK Cameras to Upgrade Security at Headquarters

Mission

Mobarakeh Steel Company (MSC), located near the city of Mobaraken, Esfahan Province in Iran, is the country's largest steelmaker, and one of its biggest industrial conglomerates.

Due to new business initiatives, MSC needed to greatly enhance security at its headquarters, and moreover, required that the cameras in a new IP camera-based surveillance system be installed within two days. MSC worked with system integrator FARAD Rayaneh Pardaz for the project.

Challenges

According to an account manager from the system integrator, VIVOTEK was MSC's first choice for camera vendor because of their excellent quality and reasonable cost.

However, a total of 139 VIVOTEK cameras supplied by distributor Pouya Fara Negar need to be installed in the MSC headquarters building by FARAD Rayaneh Pardaz in a mere two days, according to MSC's challenging timetable.

Solution

Because it was impossible to design and implement a network encompassing all these new cameras in such a short timespan, FARAD Rayaneh Pardaz's technical team decided to install the cameras as stand-alone units first, taking advantage of their SD card slots to record surveillance video on removable storage. The cameras were subsequently connected via an optical fiber network and wireless LAN to MSC's security control center for monitoring and recording.

VIVOTEK's FD8161 was chosen for offices, the warehouse, and locker room because of its excellent image quality and PIR sensor for detecting motion. IP8161 cameras were installed in areas just around the headquarters building, including the entrances, and at various bank ATMs located inside. This model was chosen because it combined high resolutions with efficient H.264 video compression that enables the transmission of surveillance video over limited network bandwidth while preserving picture quality. Other models like the IP7161, FD7160 and PZ7122 were installed in various other locations around MSC headquarters.

Customer Feedback

According to FARAD Rayaneh Pardaz, MSC's new VIVOTEK camera-based security system has performed ably despite its very rushed beginnings. Besides the superb image quality that the company's security administrator had already anticipated due to his previous experience with VIVOTEK solutions, features like the SD card support have proven their worth.
