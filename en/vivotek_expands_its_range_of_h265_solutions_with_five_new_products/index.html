VIVOTEK Expands its Range of H.265 Solutions with Five New Products

2016.10.06

Taipei, Taiwan –VIVOTEK, a global IP surveillance provider, today announced that it will add five new H.265/HEVC surveillance products to its current portfolio. These include one zoom bullet network camera, IZ9361-EH, two full-HD speed dome network cameras SD9161-H and SD9363-EHL, and two 5-megapixel fisheye network cameras FE9182-H and FE9382-EHV. These new products will allow VIVOTEK to complete its line of H.265/HEVC solutions with 24 diversified types of network cameras, 4 network video recorders, and advanced professional VMS (Video Management Software), VAST. All products utilize VIVOTEK’s Smart Stream II technology, which can reduce both bandwidth and storage consumption by up to 80% while offering the highest standards of image quality.

To begin with, the new H.265 outdoor zoom lens camera, IZ9361-EH, is designed to support outdoor long-range surveillance monitoring. Equipped with 150-meter IR illuminators and a 20x optical zoom lens, the IZ9361-EH provides supreme and real-time images around the clock. By adopting VIVOTEK's latest VAIR (Vari-Angle IR) technology, the camera also automatically adjusts the IR angle of the IR illuminators to match the camera’s FOV (Field Of View), allowing longer distance and highly uniform IR intensity. Further, armed with an IP67, IK10, and NEMA 4X -rated housing, the IZ9361-EH can offer a superior level of protection against rain, dust, and corrosion found in extreme environments.

VIVOTEK also introduces 2 new speed-dome network cameras, the indoor SD9161-H and outdoor SD9363-EHL. The indoor SD9161-H delivers 1080p full HD resolution with a 10x optical zoom lens, and features the added maneuverability of a 360° continuous pan and 90° tilt. It can capture detailed video footage and is especially suitable for wide-open indoor spaces, such as airports and shopping malls, where the highest levels of reliability and precision are required. The SD9363-EHL is ideal for outdoor applications, featuring a 20x optical zoom lens and VAIR (Vari-Angle IR) technology, it will capture fine details at top-notch quality, 24 hours a day. Its robust construction is further strengthened with an IP66, IK10 and NEMA 4X-rated housing for rugged protection. These two cameras incorporate advanced features such as WDR Pro, EIS (Electronic Image Stabilization) to control image stability, auto tracking and a defog function, making them the perfect choice for indoor or outdoor surveillance needs.

Finally, two 5-megapixel fisheye network cameras also debuted in this launch, the FE9182-H and FE9382-EHV. Both are equipped with a removable IR-cut filter, 20-meter IR illuminators and WDR Pro technology, enabling the cameras to maintain optimal image quality and unparalleled visibility in high contrast lighting environments. Moreover, the FE9382-EHV is designed with full EN50155 compliance, vandal proof IK10-rated, and weather-proof IP66-rated housing, ensuring that the camera can withstand shock, vibration, humidity, dust, and temperature fluctuations, and maintain stable and reliable video capture during any vehicle movement. These latest two models are scheduled to be available this December.

For more information about VIVOTEK and its comprehensive product lines, please visit www.vivotek.com.

About VIVOTEK

VIVOTEK, established in 2000, has quickly taken its place as a leading brand in the security industry. Known for delivering world-class IP surveillance solutions, VIVOTEK specializes in system applications and integration. With innovative R&D teams adopting ground-breaking codec technologies, VIVOTEK provides a wide range of products, including network cameras, video servers, video receivers, network video recorders, central management software, and PoE solutions. VIVOTEK (TAIEX: 3454) was listed on the Taiwan Stock Exchange in 2011. In 2008, a subsidiary was set up in California, USA. And European subsidiary was established in Almere, the Netherlands in 2013, India office in Delhi in 2014, Middle East subsidiary in Dubai in 2015, and Mexico office in 2016. VIVOTEK is currently working with more than 183 authorized distributors in over 116 countries. For more information, please visit www.vivotek.com.
