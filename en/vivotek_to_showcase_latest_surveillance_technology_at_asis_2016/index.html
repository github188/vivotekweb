VIVOTEK to Showcase Latest Surveillance Technology at ASIS 2016

2016.09.12

San Jose, Calif. – September 1, 2016 – VIVOTEK USA, Inc. a leading manufacturer in the network video surveillance industry, will be featuring an extensive range of surveillance solutions throughout the 62nd annual ASIS International Seminar & Exhibits in Orlando, Florida, booth 2767 at the Orange County Convention Center on September 12th to the 14th. Drawing more than 20,000 security professionals from 97 countries, ASIS is the most comprehensive educational and networking event in the security industry. Known as one of the largest and most dedicated shows for security professionals worldwide, ASIS offers the perfect opportunity to keep up with market trends and new products, shaping the future of the industry.

VIVOTEK’s booth at ASIS will demonstrate its latest technology, including high efficiency video codec H.265, applications for retail and transportation, as well as panoramic solutions. Improved speed domes, zoom bullets and a new corner camera will be available for exhibition by VIVOTEK staff at booth 2767.

  • Retail/People Counting Solution: VIVOTEK’s wide range of options for retail applications now includes a people counting solution to help business owners and managers improve operational efficiency and increase profitability. VIVOTEK’s people counting solution is integrated with VIVOTEK’s VAST CMS, providing reliable in-store analytics and around-the-clock surveillance.

  • Speed Dome: VIVOTEK’s high performance speed dome network cameras SD9161-H, SD9364-EH, and SD9365-EHL offer 1080p full HD resolution and consistently deliver superb image quality. SD9161-H is a high-performance H.265 speed dome network camera, which includes power of a 10x optical zoom lens and a refined pan/tilt mechanism, suitable for capturing fine detail in wide-open indoor spaces such as airports and lobbies. SD9364-EH is specifically designed to enhance low light surveillance in large coverage areas. Equipped with 250M IR illuminators and a 30x optical zoom lens, the SD9364-EH provides superior low light images in the most challenging situations. VIVOTEK’s SD9365-EHL Outdoor Speed Dome Network camera comes equipped with wiper technology suitable for inclement weather conditions, ensuring a clear picture is captured 24 hours, 7 days a week.

  • H.265 Bandwidth & Storage Saving: Consistently delivering reliable and quality products, VIVOTEK continues to launch new solutions in the IP surveillance industry. Revealing the new V-Pro H.265 line of end-to-end options, with network cameras, including 3 and 5-Megapixel camera models, NVR’s, and central management software, VAST, all components are compatible with H.265 compression technology. VIVOTEK’s new V-Pro line enables higher and more efficient image compression rates than previous H.264 systems while maintaining high image quality. In addition to H.265 hardware, utilizing VIVOTEK’s Smart Stream II video compression technology, the V-Pro H.265 series of cameras reduce bandwidth and storage consumption by up to 80%, making a significant reduction in network bandwidth and storage requirements.

VIVOTEK USA invites you to visit their booth, #2767 at ASIS International in Orlando, FL, September 13th to the 14th. Explore VIVOTEK’s full spectrum of solutions, visit for demonstrations and engage with knowledgeable sales representatives from the East Coast as well as Central area of the United States.

About VIVOTEK

VIVOTEK, established in 2000, has quickly taken its place as a leading brand in the security industry. Known for delivering world-class IP surveillance solutions, VIVOTEK specializes in system applications and integration. With innovative R&D teams adopting ground-breaking codec technologies, VIVOTEK provides a wide range of products, including network cameras, video servers, video receivers, network video recorders, central management software, and PoE solutions. VIVOTEK (TAIEX: 3454) was listed on the Taiwan Stock Exchange in 2011. In 2008, a subsidiary was set up in California, USA. And European subsidiary was established in Almere, the Netherlands in 2013, India office in Delhi in 2014, Middle East subsidiary in Dubai in 2015, and Mexico office in 2016. VIVOTEK is currently working with more than 183 authorized distributors in over 116 countries. For more information, please visit www.vivotek.com.
