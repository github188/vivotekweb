VIVOTEK Showcases Advanced Transportation Solutions at INNOTRANS 2016

2016.08.15

Taipei, Taiwan –VIVOTEK, the leading IP surveillance provider, announced today that it will demonstrate its transportation solutions at the INNOTRANS 2016 taking place from September 20 to 23 at the Berlin Exhibition Grounds. Located at Hall 4-1 booth No.105, VIVOTEK will highlight a variety of the latest surveillance technologies focused on enabling station operators to acquire and distribute intelligence for strategic management and immediate appropriate response.

Visitors can experience advanced security solutions designed especially for three scenarios, including Railway and Subway Cars, Outdoor Railroad Grade Crossings, and Station Lobbies.

  • Railway and Subway Cars：In order to enhance surveillance performance for diversified transportation applications, VIVOTEK is excited to introduce a line-up of diversified mobile dome cameras and an industrial EN50155 M12 PoE switch for railway and subway cars. With built-in WDR Pro technology, the new compact mobile dome camera, MD8563-EH provides improved visibility in high-contrast lighting environments, such as at the entrance of rail or subway cars. Packed with powerful features, the camera features a full EN50155 Class Tx compliance, IK10 and IP67-rated rugged design and will withstand shocks, vibration, humidity, dust, and temperature fluctuations and maintain stable and reliable video capture during jarring vehicle movement. And the AW-IHT-0602 industrial EN50155 M12 PoE switch allows users to deploy IP surveillance in transportation applications at ease with its M12 connection design and support a wide range of operating temperature ranging from -40°C to 75°C.

  • Outdoor Railroad Grade Crossings：Promoting grade-crossing safety is an essential aspect of the drive to reduce accidents. Realizing this, VIVOTEK introduces the new H.265 outdoor zoom lens camera, IZ9361-EH to support the monitoring of outdoor grade crossings. Equipped with 150m IR illuminators, and a 20x optical zoom lens, the IZ9361-EH provides supreme and real-time images of the flashing red lights and gates around the grade crossings. Further armed with an IP67, NEMA 4X- and IK10-rated housing, the IZ9361-EH can offer a superior level of protection from corrosion and from extreme environments. Increasing both the flexibility and stability of the IZ9361-EH, operators can opt for an outdoor-ready IP66 and IK10-rated cabinet with power supply, industrial PoE device, fiber media converter, and surge protection kit, all of which combine to offer a complete outdoor surveillance solution.

  • Station Lobbies：Attuned to growing demands for station security, VIVOTEK has developed the three H.265 IR speed dome cameras, including SD9363-EHL, SD9364-EH, and SD9364-EHL. Equipped with a 20x/30x optical zoom lens, 150/250 meters IR illuminators and VIVOTEK's latest VAIR (Vari-Angle IR) technology, allowing both a brighter night view and more consistent illumination. These cameras are designed with a sophisticated pan/tilt mechanism with continuous 360-degree pan and 220-degree tilt. Security staffs can thus monitor and track any object of interest in the lobby via a mouse or a joystick with ease. Also on display will be the one of a kind people counting solution, SC8131, featuring VIVOTEK’s 3D Depth Technology that empowers accurate measuring of visitor traffic in the main entrances of a station around the clock. Providing safety and security for passengers and visitors is paramount to all station operators. With VIVOTEK, new technology can secure the safety of passengers more than ever before.

About VIVOTEK

VIVOTEK, established in 2000, has quickly taken its place as a leading brand in the security industry. Known for delivering world-class IP surveillance solutions, VIVOTEK specializes in system applications and integration. With innovative R&D teams adopting ground-breaking codec technologies, VIVOTEK provides a wide range of products, including network cameras, video servers, video receivers, network video recorders, central management software, and PoE solutions. VIVOTEK (TAIEX: 3454) was listed on the Taiwan Stock Exchange in 2011. In 2008, a branch office was set up in California, USA. VIVOTEK established Europe branch office in Almere, the Netherlands in 2013, India office in Delhi in 2014, and Middle East office in Dubai in 2015, Mexico office in 2016. VIVOTEK is currently working with more than 183 authorized distributors in over 116 countries. For more information, please visit www.vivotek.com.
