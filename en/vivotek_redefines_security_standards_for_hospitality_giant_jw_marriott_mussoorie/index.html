VIVOTEK redefines security standards for hospitality giant JW Marriott Mussoorie

2016.12.06

Taipei, Taiwan - Reformulating security standards for hospitality behemoth, JW Marriott, Mussoorie, Walnut Grove Resort and Spa in India, VIVOTEK brings the property at par with security standards prevailing across the globe. Counteracting to high security prerequisites, VIVOTEK a leading brand in IP surveillance security, in association with Sysware Infotech, its system integration partner, pivoted the Analogue video surveillance consumer, into an IP surveillance consumer. Edifying its first hospitality client on the downsides of Analogue video surveillance and predicting inadequacies the brand could suffer, VIVOTEK eventually convinced the brand to also transform their security guidelines across the country.

Commenting on the successful commissioning of the IP surveillance security system, Sanjeev Gulati, Country Manager – India & SAARC, VIVOTEK Inc. commented, “This project was a significant milestone for VIVOTEK, as it was our pilot hospitality project. Sysware Infotech, our system integration partners, did a thorough survey of the entire terrain and the construction and accordingly we planted solutions. Post the deployment process, the entire commissioning was verified and then a user level training was also conducted. Only after the training, the entire facility was handed over to the in-house team.”

Yogesh Mehta, Director, Sysware Infotech Pvt. Ltd. commented, “We had to operate within a given set of guidelines – specifications and budget. Furthermore adding to our strains, were the rough terrain and the climatic conditions. We planted a total of 125 IP network cameras in the resort spread over 25 acres, on a hilly terrain that experiences extreme low temperature and snowfall.”

The products deployed in the project include vary-focal indoor dome- FD8131, ultra-mini indoor dome- FD8136, outdoor bullet camera- IP8335H, outdoor speed dome camera - SD8322, and indoor speed dome -SD8121. Each model installed, has different functionality. Post installations; the client applauded the crystal clear picture quality and ease of management with features like remote viewing etc. and also appreciated the reduced number of cables from the cameras to the control room.
