VIVOTEK to Showcase Latest Technology in Network Video Surveillance at ISC WEST

2016.04.04

San Jose, Calif. – April 4th, 2016 – VIVOTEK USA, Inc. a leading manufacturer in the network video surveillance industry, will be featuring a wide range of new products throughout the largest security convention in North America, The International Security Conference & Exposition (Booth #12089) at Sands Expo & Convention Center in Las Vegas, Nevada on April 6th through the 8th. ISC WEST security tradeshow offers the perfect opportunity to meet with many exhibitors and over 28,000 security professionals in the industry, allowing attendees to keep up with the newest security products and technology as well as the constantly changing market trends.

At ISC West, VIVOTEK will exhibit its latest technology, including ultimate bandwidth and storage saving solutions, retail and discreet form factors, the improved zoom bullet camera and VIVOTEK’s collaboration with GENETEC’s Stratocast Cloud Platform, as well as PoE solutions.

  • Bandwidth & Storage Saving: Consistently delivering reliable and quality products, VIVOTEK continues to launch new solutions in the IP surveillance industry. Revealing the new V-Pro H.265 line of end-to-end options, with network cameras, including 3 and 5-Megapixel camera models, NVR’s, and central management software, VAST, all components are compatible with H.265 compression technology. VIVOTEK’s new V-Pro line enables higher and more efficient image compression rates than previous H.264 systems while maintaining high image quality. In addition to H.265 hardware, utilizing VIVOTEK’s Smart Stream II video compression technology, the V-Pro H.265 series of cameras reduce bandwidth and storage consumption by up to 80%, making a significant reduction in network bandwidth and storage requirements.

  • Retail Solutions & Discreet Form Factors: VIVOTEK’s wide range of options for retail and discreet form factor include the people counting stereo camera, SC8131, 2MP recessed mount fixed dome network camera, FD816C-HF2, and at only 60 mm wide it features 109° horizontal field of view at 30 fps. Also on display at ISC West is VC8101 Split-type Camera System, a full HD camera system enabled with a separate camera unit and video core design with a 5-meter cable enabling installation into any interior design. The inconspicuous construction reduces the risk of tampering or vandalism, perfect for banks, retail stores, restaurants and offices. Other products ideal for these specific applications include the CC8130 (HS) 1MP Height Strip Network Camera, the 5MP FE8182 recessed mount Fisheye Network Camera provides 360° surround view without blind spots, suitable for open areas such as lobbies, malls, retail stores and offices.

  • IR Speed Dome: VIVOTEK’s new and improved S-Series of speed dome network cameras will also be demonstrated at ISC West. PTZ Speed Dome models SD9361-EHL, SD9362-EHL, SD9364-EH and SD9364-EHL are collectively high performance H.265 Full HD speed dome network cameras suitable for many applications. Equipped with a 20x optical zoom lens, SD9361-EHL offers extra smooth video quality with a resolution of up to 60 fps at 1920x1080. Also equipped with WDR Pro technology and IR-cut filter for seamless day and night operation, the SD9364-EHL is suitable for monitoring wide, open, harsh indoor or outdoor spaces such as airports, highways and parking lots where high-level reliability and precision are needed. VIVOTEK’s SD9364-EHL professional speed dome camera contains a 30x optical zoom lens and resolution of up to 60 fps at 1080p full HD. By combining 1080p full HD resolution with H.265, IR illuminators, VAIR (Vari-Angle IR), WDR, and 30x optical zoom, the SD9364-EHL is able to capture fine details with unprecedented quality, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. When combined with VIVOTEK's Smart Stream II technology users can obtain bandwidth savings of up to 80% compared to traditional H.264.

  • Zoom Bullet: VIVOTEK’s new zoom bullet cameras, IZ9361-EH and IZ9371-EH offers 60 fps at up to 30x optical zoom and provides excellent low light images in the most challenging lighting conditions. Equipped with VAIR (Vari-Angle IR), VIVOTEK’s latest IR technology, it allows broad coverage FOV and high uniform IR intensity is distributed to provide smooth adjustment of the IR illuminators allowing the capture of fast moving objects in large open areas under difficult lighting environments. Ideal for applications , where lighting is an issue such a stadiums, parking lots, airports as well as license plate recognition.

VIVOTEK invites you to visit their booth #12089 at ISC West on April 6th through the 8th in Las Vegas, Nevada at Sands Expo & Convention Center. Discover VIVOTEK’s new and comprehensive security solutions in the HD and megapixel IP surveillance industry.

About VIVOTEK

VIVOTEK, established in 2000, has quickly taken its place as a leading brand in the security industry. Known for delivering world-class IP surveillance solutions, VIVOTEK specializes in system applications and integration. With innovative R&D teams adopting ground-breaking codec technologies, VIVOTEK provides a wide range of products, including network cameras, video servers, video receivers, network video recorders, central management software, and PoE solutions. VIVOTEK (TAIEX: 3454) was listed on the Taiwan Stock Exchange in 2011. In 2008, a branch office was set up in California, USA. VIVOTEK established Europe branch office in Almere, the Netherlands in 2013, India office in Delhi in 2014, and Middle East office in Dubai in 2015. VIVOTEK is currently working with more than 183 authorized distributors in over 116 countries. For more information, please visit www.vivotek.com.
