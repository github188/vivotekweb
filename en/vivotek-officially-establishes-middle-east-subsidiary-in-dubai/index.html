VIVOTEK Officially Establishes Middle East Subsidiary in Dubai

2015.12.10

Taipei, Taiwan – VIVOTEK is pleased to announce the establishment of its subsidiary in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. Aware of the strong business growth in Middle East markets, VIVOTEK started preparing for the establishment of a subsidiary in this region in 2014. Dubai, with its friendly investment environment and location adjacent to several Middle East countries, was chosen as the base for supporting VIVOTEK’s expansion in Middle East.

William Ku, Vice President, Brand Business Division, VIVOTEK Inc., remarked “For years, business revenue generated from the Middle East Market has made significant contribution to VIVOTEK’s overall revenue. We see this region as vital for VIVOTEK’s growing future. Dubai, with a unique geographic location in Middle East, is a perfect hub on the continent and, without a doubt, is an ideal place for companies setting up subsidiary for business development in this market. We are truly excited about the establishment of our Dubai subsidiary, which we believe will make business communication, transactions, sales, and support more efficient and allow VIVOTEK to offer even greater value to our customers. We expect even greater growth following this expansion in the region.”

VIVOTEK’s subsidiary is situated in Jabel Ali free zone; an ideal location for VIVOTEK to store, manage and dispatch products. The establishment of this subsidiary will bring immediate benefits to our customers in this market, such as lower, or even zero taxes, faster response time, closer and more agile services support and shorter shipment time. At the current initial phase, the subsidiary is of a modest size. A full and comprehensive facility will be completed in the near future.
