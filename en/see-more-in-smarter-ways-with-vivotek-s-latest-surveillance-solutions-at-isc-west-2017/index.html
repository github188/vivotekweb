See More in Smarter Ways with VIVOTEK’s latest Surveillance Solutions at ISC WEST 2017

  3. See More in Smarter Ways with VIVOTEK’s latest Surveillance Solutions at ISC WEST 2017

2017-04-03

San Jose, CA – April 3rd, 2017 – VIVOTEK USA, Inc. the global leading IP surveillance solution provider, will showcase a wide range of new products throughout the largest security convention in North America, The International Security Conference & Exposition (Booth #12089) at Sands Expo & Convention Center in Las Vegas, Nevada on April 5th through the 7th.

At ISC West, VIVOTEK will exhibit its latest technology, including ultimate bandwidth and storage saving solutions, retail and discreet form factors, a wide-range of 180° and 360° panoramic solutions, advanced transportation applications, as well as solutions for correctional and clinical facilities.

Bandwidth & Storage Saving: VIVOTEK’s V-Pro H.265 line of end-to-end options, with network cameras, including 3 and 5-megapixel camera models, NVR’s, and central management software, VAST, all components are compatible with H.265 compression technology. The V-Pro line enables higher and more efficient image compression rates than previous H.264 systems while maintaining high image quality. In addition to H.265 hardware, utilizing VIVOTEK’s Smart Stream II video compression technology, the V-Pro H.265 series of cameras reduce bandwidth and storage consumption by up to 80%, making a significant reduction in network bandwidth and storage requirements.

Retail Solutions & Discreet Form Factors: VIVOTEK’s wide range of options for retail and discreet form factor include the people counting stereo camera, SC8131, 2-megapixel ultra-mini fixed dome network camera, FD8166A-N, and at only 90 mm wide it features built-in 940nm Invisible IR Illuminators up to 6 meters. Also on display at ISC West is VC8101 Split-type Camera System, a Full-HD camera system enabled with a separate camera unit and video core design with a 5-meter cable enabling installation into any interior design. The inconspicuous construction reduces the risk of tampering or vandalism, perfect for banks, retail stores, restaurants and offices. Other products ideal for these specific applications include the CC8160(HS) 2-megapixel Height Strip Network Camera, the 5-megapixel FE8182 recessed mount Fisheye Network Camera, measuring a diameter of only 60 mm, provides 360° surround view without blind spots, suitable for open areas such as lobbies, malls, retail stores and offices.

180° & 360° Panoramic Solutions: MS8391-EV is designed with multiple sensors providing higher resolution, maximizing the field of view and reducing the total number of cameras required, ultimately saving on installation costs. Featuring 4 x 3MP CMOS sensors, the multiple sensor network camera can monitor full horizontal views and deliver high-quality comprehensive coverage, making it an ideal outdoor surveillance solution with sharper images for more details. Covering a wide angle of 180°, the MS8391-EV provides complete video security with no dead angles. VIVOTEK will also demonstrate MS8392-EV, 12-megapixel Multiple Sensor Dome Network Camera. The MS8392-EV has a video alignment feature that allows you to adjust the positioning and image quality of each sensor. This enables you to record a seamless 180° image without distortion. In addition, the MS8392-EV is armed with a robust IP66 and IK10-rated housing to enable the multi-sensor camera to withstand rain and dust as well as to protect against vandalism or tampering. Featured at VIVOTEK’s booth will also be a wide-range of H.265 360° Panoramic Fisheye Network Cameras, including FE9181-H, FE9381-EHV and FE9382-EHV.

Advanced Transportation Applications: A network camera series equipped with an M12 connection and EN50155 Tx standard compliance for railway systems, the MD8563-EH maintains stable and reliable video output during movement. Designed with an IP67-rated rugged design, the shockproof, dustproof, waterproof and temperature resistant camera can withstand tough environments as well as provide improved visibility in high-contrast lighting environments with WDR Pro. For the interiors of locomotives, the 5-megapixel fisheye fixed dome network camera, FE8181V delivers 360° surround view without blind spots.

Correctional and Clinical Facilities: VIVOTEK also introduces the launch of CD8371-HNTV, the latest 3-megapixel vandal proof IR corner dome network camera specifically designed for installation in the upper corner of a small room. Equipped with inconspicuous and invisible 940nm IR Illuminators, 24/7 surveillance is undetectable. Available in vari-focal (-HNTV) or fixed-focal (-HNVF2) lens, the feature-rich CD8371 is perfect for correctional facilities, holding cells, clinical and medical facilities by providing fine detail in high security environments.

VIVOTEK invites you to visit our booth #12089 at ISC West on April 5th through the 7th in Las Vegas, Nevada at Sands Expo & Convention Center. Discover VIVOTEK’s new and comprehensive security solutions in the HD and megapixel IP surveillance industry.

For more information about VIVOTEK and its comprehensive product lines, please visit: www.vivotek.com.

About VIVOTEK

VIVOTEK, established in 2000, has quickly taken its place as a leading brand in the security industry. Known for delivering world-class IP surveillance solutions, VIVOTEK specializes in system applications and integration. With innovative R&D teams adopting ground-breaking codec technologies, VIVOTEK provides a wide range of products, including network cameras, video servers, video receivers, network video recorders, central management software, and PoE solutions. VIVOTEK (TAIEX: 3454) was listed on the Taiwan Stock Exchange in 2011. In 2008, a subsidiary was set up in California, USA. VIVOTEK established Europe subsidiary in Almere, the Netherlands in 2013, India branch office in Delhi in 2014, and Middle East subsidiary in Dubai in 2015, Mexico branch office in 2016. VIVOTEK is currently working with more than 183 authorized distributors in over 116 countries. For more information, please visit www.vivotek.com.
