VIVOTEK to Showcase Latest Total Surveillance Solution at IFSEC 2015

2015.06.15

Taipei, Taiwan - VIVOTEK, a total surveillance solution provider, is proud to attend IFSEC International 2015, the grand festival for the security industry. IFSEC 2015 will be held at the ExCeL London during June 16-18. VIVOTEK warmly invites you to visit the VIVOTEK booth (No. E555) and experience a range of innovative and intelligent network surveillance solutions.

VIVOTEK will present a series of latest solutions at IFSEC 2015, including:

Panoramic Solutions:

A comprehensive range of fisheye cameras, ranging from the Ultra-Megapixel fisheye FE8191 and FE8391-V equipped with 12MP CMOS sensor, and the IR fisheye FE8181V to the world's smallest fisheye, the FE8180.

Retail Solution:

The ultra-mini dome network cameras FD8168, an eye-level height strip camera CC8130(HS) and the world's smallest fisheye, the FE8180.

NVR Solution:

The embedded Plug & Play NVR ND8322P with the 2 MP bullet-style IR camera IB8369 for outdoor use and the dome-style IR camera FD8169 for indoor use.

City Surveillance Solutions:

The world's first Daisy-Chain PoE outdoor bullet IP camera IB8367-R and the professional 2-Megapixel box network camera IP816A-HP.

VivoCam PoE Solutions:

The world's first L2 PoE Gigabit switches with IP surveillance management functions, VivoCam, features unique graphic management by topology, floor plan and Google map.

H.265 Solutions:

Moreover, VIVOTEK's close cooperation and strategic integration with global leading software partners, AxxonSoft and Genetec, and the development of H.265 solutions and VIVOTEK's camera IP9171-HP will be highlighted at booth.

Come visit the VIVOTEK booth and explore more.
