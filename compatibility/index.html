ONVIF IP Camera Support List


In order to support IP cameras through ONVIF integration, VIVOTEK provide the ONVIF client solution to support IP cameras of renowned brands in VIVOTEK’s VAST and NVRs.

O All VAST & NVR Support
O VAST VAST Support Only
O NVR NVR Support Only
Feature Support Scope Note
Multi-stream Maximum up to 4 streams VIVOTEK VAST and NVR only support one channel stream from other brand devices such as multi-sensor cameras, Video Servers, NVR's and DVR's.
Video H.264, MPEG4 & MJPEG The compression support is follow the product specification definition
Audio G.711 Only support the one way audio
PTZ Direction (Home↑↓←→) & Zoom Control Only Support the PTZ Panel Control, not support Joystick
Configuration Codec, Resolution, FPS, Intra frame period Not inculde the video quality setting
