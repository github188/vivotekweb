Overview

VIVOTEK FD8367-TV is a robust, outdoor-style network camera designed for diverse outdoor applications. Equipped with a 2MP sensor enabling viewing resolution of 1920x1080 at a smooth 30 fps, the FD8367-TV is an all-in-one outdoor camera capable of capturing high quality and high resolution video up to 2 Megapixel, even in low light environment. Providing such a polished image quality, FD8367-TV is also designed with VIVOTEK Smart Stream, 3DNR to save notable bandwidth for users.

To meet outdoor applications, FD8367-TV is also designed with IP66-rated housing to help the camera body withstand rain and dust and high pressure water jets from any direction while its IK10-rated housing can provide the protection against the vandal act and impact.To help adjust the focus more efficiently, FD8367-TV is also designed with VIVOTEK's Smart Focus System to make installation and adjustment easier by using remote focus and zoom adjustment.

Key Features

  • 2-Megapixel CMOS Sensor
  • 30 fps @ 1920x1080
  • 2.8 ~ 12 mm Vari-focal, P-iris Lens
  • Removable IR-cut Filter for Day & Night Function
  • Built-in IR Illuminators, Effective up to 30 Meters
  • Real-time H.264, and MJPEG Compression
  • Two-way Audio
  • WDR Enhancement for Unparalleled Visibility in Extremely Bright and Dark Environments
  • Weather-proof IP66-rated and Vandal-proof IK10-rated Housing
  • 3D Noise Reduction
  • Smart IR Technology to Avoid Overexposure
  • Support Installation with AM-712 Conduit Box
  • SNV (Supreme Night Visibility)
