About Lensec

Since 1998, LENSEC has been breaking new ground in video surveillance security systems. LENSEC pioneered IP video management and IP video surveillance by creating real-time situational awareness tools designed and delivered for the web. Focusing on functionality as well as the user experience, LENSEC solutions -- which include web-based video surveillance and network camera surveillance software -- have become the most relied upon video surveillance systems for first responders and emergency security personnel.

With over 30,000 camera installations in over 30 states, LENSEC's expertise delivers solutions that increase safety, reduce total operating cost, and allow more effective remote management of all public safety operations.
