ABOUT Ecolumina

EcoLumina Technologies, Inc. (ETI) founded in May, 2008 with a mission to develop the most advanced and environment-friendly energy efficient lighting products. Since its inception, ETI has devoted its resource to the science, technology and art in the fields of light and lighting developing the cornerstone product such as solar matrix management system, power control system, and LED control/dimming techniques. In 2012, ETI joined the Smart City Public Lighting Development Program, created “CosWe”, a total IoT (Internet of Things) solution by combining cloud control, lighting system and various kinds of sensors. In 2015, ETI brings video security solution into the CosWe system. ETI’s goal is to continuously provide complete end-to-end IoT solution to the market.

ETI is an R&D-driven, customer-oriented company aimed at attaining 100% customer satisfaction. We strive to continually exceed expectations, delivering excellence to our clients and partners – our most precious assets.
