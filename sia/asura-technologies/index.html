About ASURA Technologies

Asura Technologies is driven by a single idea – to make LPR software integration simple. The Asura team excels at creating user friendly applications for integrators that makes LPR integration as easy as installing an app on your smartphone.

Integrators waste an excessive amount of money, time and personnel to integrate leading LPR technology into their systems. Asura brings you the new generation of LPR integration – a turnkey solution which allows the integration of the OCR engine into any LPR system.

In our opinion, an LPR system has to be made by a software developer company. LPR systems should be hardware-independent, and that is the independence Asura Technologies gives you. Great camera manufacturers should do what they are good at, providing us with the video stream.
