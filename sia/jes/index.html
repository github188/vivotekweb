About JES Hardware Solutions

Founded in 1992, JES Hardware Solutions is an Native American owned and Florida Certified MBE (OSD), Florida FRMBC and a self-certified SDB company based in Hollywood, FL, providing complex and custom computer engineering solutions systems to various government prime contractors and private sector business. While working with companies such as Lockheed Martin, L3 Communications and Boeing, JES has produced proficient computer storage systems to suit our clients project needs, sometimes providing the most bleeding edge technologies to its customers. JES can do this because of its many alliances with major storage and storage infrastructure companies and in most cases can get pre-market release products.

The company works directly with engineers and buyers by preparing estimates and in solution implementation.
