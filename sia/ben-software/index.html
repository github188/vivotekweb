ABOUT Ben Software

Bensoftware was founded by Ben Bird in the year 2000 with the sole purpose of providing high-quality video-related software for the Mactintosh platform. Our first video capture software, BTV, proved very popular and is now widely used. In 2004 we brought out our video surveillance software SecuritySpy to positive customer response and have been continually improving it since then.

We are based in London, UK, but our products are available online worldwide.
