About Videonetics

A fast-growing Indian pioneer with a long-term vision, Videonetics leverages its innovative, cutting-edge and indigenous technology to help its customers achieve better return on investment. Videonetics technology is based on a solid foundation of ground-breaking intellectual property.

Videonetics designs, develops and provides products such as Intelligent VMS integrated with video analytics software, Intelligent Traffic Management System, Facial Recognition, Cloud VMS & Analytics, Video Summarization etc. to power video surveillance systems. Videonetics visual computing platform has been developed based on the principle of green computing with small computational and memory footprint, and can process more number of video channels compared to the competition. These innovative offerings can substantially reduce operating and capital costs for a video surveillance system while improving its overall performance and reliability
