About PlateSmart

PlateSmart has developed the world’s first software-only security solution and remains the leading provider of camera-agnostic Vehicle Identification Recognition (VRI) and Intelligent Video Analytics solutions. PlateSmart offers both mobile, fixed, and remote VRI and analytics solutions, which are designed either to function as stand-alone tools or to integrate with third-party software and hardware. ARES Insights is the world’s first and most advanced analytic solution with Business Intelligence capabilities for vehicle and alert information analysis.

ARES, PlateSmart’s enterprise vehicle recognition and analytic solution provides real-time actionable intelligence including full color image, plate number, vehicle make, state jurisdiction, GPS coordinates and date and time stamped for complete situational awareness. ARES advantage is the ability to capture more data and deliver more actionable intelligence from a wider field of view with Object Recognition technology, instead of the industry standard and limited OCR technology.

PlateSmart’s open-architecture allows for seamless integration with third-party software and is designed to function as a stand-alone solution. PlateSmart serves enterprise, government, and commercial organizations through our ecosystem of nationally recognized security solution providers. PlateSmart has been recognized year-over-year from industry trade publications and has received many awards for their patent-pending ARES enterprise solution. They have been recognized as the most innovative and forward-thinking ALPR technology by Frost and Sullivan.
