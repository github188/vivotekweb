About Suretek

Suretek is an innovative security technology company operating in the heart of Sydney's fastest growing industrial corridor, Wetherill Park.

We are leaders in the development of new technologies such as GPRS monitoring systems and networks, high priority security telecommunication solutions, as well as back-up and redundancy systems.

We also specialise heavily in security software, hardware and installation for monitoring centres in Australia, New Zealand, Asia and America.
