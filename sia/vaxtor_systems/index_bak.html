About Vaxtor Systems

Vaxtor Systems –ANPR Technology, Products and Solutions provider

Vaxtor Systems is a leading ANPR solutions provider with its own OCR technology and a clear international outlook. We are specialized in delivering products with cutting edge technology in the most demanding areas like high speed highways, onboard solutions, smart cities or smartphone applications.

We count with a proprietary, resilient, and platform independent OCR Engine. All our products share the same technology, allowing us to improve our capabilities with each new release. Vaxtor OCR Engine has been specifically designed and developed to operate under both normal and harsh environment.
