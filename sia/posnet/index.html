ABOUT Posnet

Posnet Polska S.A is a Polish company with over 20 years of experience in POS devices market. Posnet is a manufacturer of cash registers and POS printers, as well as provider of solutions for sales and services, like scales, bar code scanners, touchscreens. Posnet's leading line of products is 'Thermal' cash register series.
