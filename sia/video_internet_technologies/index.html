ABOUT Video Internet Technologies

Video Internet Technologies is a leading Ukrainian company that develops unique and innovative ITS Solutions (Intelligent Transportation Systems) for the automatic road enforcement purposes. The products (both software and hardware) designed by VIT are used all over the world and proved its efficiency and reliability in over 10000 installations.

VIT provides all-inclusive project implementation: starting from the development of the devices and the software, their integration with third parties components and up to incorporation of all mentioned components into one system.
