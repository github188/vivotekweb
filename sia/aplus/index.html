About A-Plus Video Technologies

A-Plus Video Technologies, Inc., widely recognized for its solid Linux R&D capabilities, provides leading edge hybrid video recorder and VMS software total solutions which leverage the power of mobile devices, wireless telecommunications and broadband networking. Utilizing a Linux-embedded kernel in conjunction with HD video compression, analysis and streaming, its engineering team has developed a wide range of hybrid HD surveillance solutions which are highly robust and completely stable. As a professional solution provider dedicated to improving the quality and reliability of high definition video surveillance, we use our unique resources to complement the efforts of our customers.
