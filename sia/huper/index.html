About Huper Laboratories

Huper Laboratories (known as HuperLab) is a leading developer of intelligent video surveillance solutions, and a technology innovator on 2D & 3D video analytics, video/audio codec and streaming technologies. Founded in 1997, huperLab has developed to offering a well-designed platform that allows any connection of HD-SDI, Analog, Ultra 960H and Megapixel IP cameras, with high quality video display, recording and remote monitoring functions on mobile phones and tablets. The self-developed and patent granted 2D & 3D video analytics can further be added onto this platform for the use of security surveillance and business intelligence in vertical markets. With over 15 years of experiences in research and development, huperLab brings video analytics to the new era by integrating stereo vision technology, which delivers more accurate analysis results with less influence of shadows and light changes. Till now huperLab's video analytics have been installed and recognized by customers from different verticals worldwide.
