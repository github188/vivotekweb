About SKYROS Corporation

SKYROS is a system integrator and software/hardware development company that provides reliable security products with outstanding service to satisfy customers' needs. The Company designs, develops, manufactures and markets a range of CCTV and Access Control turnkey solutions for over 18 years (since 1995) for the Russian and CIS market.

SKYROS has obtained the authorization to be a distributor for the high-class security products of the leading European and South-East Asian manufacturers of Intercoms, Access Control, CCTV and Fire Alarms systems and established a broad efficient dealer network, thus stimulating sales driven by total customer satisfaction and repeat business. The Company's primary target markets include, but not limited to, the OEMs, building and installation enterprises, trade houses, wholesalers and private households.

VideoNet® - is a powerful multifunctional video management system that gives a possibility to create various solutions for video surveillance systems for different applications and objects of different scale. It also provides you with a possibility of total control and monitoring of the whole video surveillance system from every point of the video network.

Flexible modular structure and supreme technical characteristics together with broad functional capabilities, ease of use and reliability, in aggregate with a full range of services such as technical support, maintenance, after-sales service and warranty package that are provided by producer have assured VideoNet system wide popularity and expansion within and outside the borders of the territory of Russian Federation.
