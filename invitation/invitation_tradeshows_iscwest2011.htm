Dear VIVOTEK Partner and Customer:

VIVOTEK USA would like to cordially invite you to the ISC West 2011 exhibition held in Las Vegas, Nevada, USA from April 6 to 8, 2011.

Leading the way at VIVOTEK's booth this year will be the launch of our SUPREME series of network cameras, designed as the convergence of HD and megapixel technology with advanced features and reliability. We are especially excited to showcase the North American debut of the FE8171E camera, featuring 360° panoramic view in astounding 3-Megapixel resolution that will change the face of IP surveillance.

Among the technologies being demonstrated as part of the SUPREME series, Supreme Night Visibility cameras offer unparalleled visibility under low light conditions. Focus Assist and Remote Focus, P-Iris, 60 fps framerate, and WDR Enhancement all offer substantial improvements in image tuning and quality, whileExtended Temperature Range offers increased camera reliability under harsh outdoor conditions.

A demonstration of key applications using VIVOTEK cameras will be available, including people counting and retail, while an IT-focused solution with video servers, NVRs, and professional series VAST opens a world of possibilities in next-generation security with a true end-to-end solution from VIVOTEK.

Be sure to stop by our presentation area, where talks will be given on an hourly basis, allowing attendees to learn about how exciting new technologies and can be utilized in applications. VIVOTEK team members will be highlighting the advantages of SUPREME series cameras, while software partners will also be on hand to present their solutions as they relate to VIVOTEK's line of cameras.

For more information or any inquires, please contact us. We look forward to seeing you there!
