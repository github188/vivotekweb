This year's Essen, VIVOTEK is proud to present our new products and technologies!

Panoramic PTZ and Smart Stream are two of the latest technologies to be implemented on VIVOTEK cameras. Panoramic PTZ (Pan/Tilt/Zoom) incorporates the fisheye camera's global view to detect events in a wide-open area when a speed dome is triggered to perform a physical pan/tilt/zoom function and track objects that correspond to the region of interests (ROI). On the other hand, Smart Stream allows users to set ROIs for improved clarity without raising the bit-rate so as to optimize storage and bandwidth efficiency.

VIVOTEK will also be exhibiting solutions for banking and retail vertical markets. A series of WDR-embedded cameras will come in handy for the entrance of bank, where the issue of high contrast lighting happens. The compact and petite indoor domes are perfect for retail applications as they are not only bandwidth-efficient but also designed to blend in well with the interior design.
