Overview

VIVOTEK IP8133 is an easy-to-use network camera specifically designed for home security applications with a compact, stylish exterior. With a camera with a 1-megapixel sensor enabling viewing resolution of 1280x800 at 30 fps, users need to look no further for an all-in-one camera capable of capturing high quality, high resolution video.

Embedded with the multimedia SoC, the camera supports the industry-standard H.264 compression technology, drastically reducing file sizes and conserving valuable network bandwidth. With MPEG-4 and MJPEG compatibility also included, video streams can also be transmitted in any of these formats for versatile applications. The streams can also be individually configured to meet different constraints, thereby further reducing bandwidth and storage requirements. Users can thus receive multiple streams simultaneously in different resolutions, frame rates, and image qualities for viewing on different platforms.

With this network camera, a user can quickly and easily view children, the elderly, or even pets with live, clear videos while away from home. The camera also includes tamper detection and VIVOTEK's 32-channel recording software. With all this and more, the IP8133 is the ideal solution for your surveillance needs.

Key Features

  • 1-megapixel CMOS Sensor
  • Real-time H.264, MPEG-4 and MJPEG compression (Triple Codec)
  • Privacy Button to Pause Monitoring
  • Tamper Detection for Unauthorized Changes
  • Built-in PIR Sensor for Human Detection
  • Built-in White-light Illuminators
  • Built-in 802.3af Compliant PoE
  • Built-in Microphone and Speaker for Two-way Audio
  • Compact, Elegant Design
