VIVOTEK announces major presence at Intersec 2015 in Dubai

VIVOTEK Inc., a leading manufacturer in the network video surveillance industry, will be featuring a wide range of products throughout the largest security event in the world, Intersec 2015 (Stand: S1-K26 Hall: S1) at the Dubai World Trade Center on January 18th - 20th. Intersec Dubai is the largest and leading tradeshow for the safety and security industry worldwide. Last year's exhibit featured 1,213 exhibitors and 24,766 visitors from 131 countries, making this trade show the largest and most diverse in the security and safety markets, allowing attendees to stay up-to-date with the latest technology.

VIVOTEK has many innovative solutions and products to share with industry leaders around the world. In 2015, VIVOTEK has redefined what the total solution is and hopes to continue to add innovative products to revolutionize the security and surveillance industry. VIVOTEK's booth will contain demos of its latest technology including FE8181, the 5-Megapixel IR Fisheye camera with built-in IR LED's and patent protected light cup design provide uniform 360° surrounding illumination for clear viewing up to 10 meters. Network Video Recorder ND8321 supplies easy set-up, live view, playback and archive search accessible by any smart phone with updated EZConnect application. The P2P (peer to peer) network connection service allows the user to easily connect to NVR ND8321 without going through complicated network settings. VIVTOEK will also showcase IB8367, the world's first IP camera with embedded PoE repeater along with other PoE solutions including the High Power DIP Switch (AW-FED-0500-065). The embedded PoE repeater allows cameras to be daisy chained and share one single power source, extending PoE distance to up to 300 meters and significantly reduce cabling cost.

Added to VIVOTEK's total and smarter solution are new features such as Video Content Analysis (VCA) including people counting, field detection and line crossing detection. VIVOTEK's professional central management software VAST provides scalable video management solution to projects of any size.

One of the most impressive innovations is the world's first L2 managed switch designed for IP surveillance. The switch automatically detects all VIVOTEK devices (video server, cameras and NVRs) and indicates important data including the topology, IP / MAC addresses and even the power consumption of each unit, allowing easier and more effective overall system maintenance.

VIVOTEK invites you to visit their stand S1-K26 Hall S1 at Intersec Dubai on January 18th - 20th. Learn more and explore first handedly the advancements VIVOTEK has made in the IP surveillance industry by constantly providing leading security solutions.
