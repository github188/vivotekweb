2017 Taiwan Excellence Awards Winner

VIVOTEK is delighted to announce that six of its intelligent IP surveillance products have received the 2017 Taiwan Excellence Awards. The products recognized include three H.264 models MS8391-EV, MD8563-EH and APC (Automatic People Counting) camera as well as the H.265 models, IZ9361-EH, FE9381-EHV and SD9364-EH. Among these six products, the SD9364-EH speed dome camera won the Taiwan Excellence Silver standard award.

INTERSEC Dubai 2017

VIVOTEK revealed innovative vertical IP surveillance solutions at the INTERSEC 2017, taking place from January 22 to 24 at Dubai International Convention and Exhibition Centre. The top spot in VIVOTEK’s booth was the intelligent retail solution with the latest 3D depth people counting technology.
