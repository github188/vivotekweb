Partnership with Genetec™ Stratocast™

VIVOTEK announces its further strategic cooperation with Genetec Inc. on its cloud-based video surveillance solution Stratocast™. Compatible with VIVOTEK’s enterprise-level network cameras, Stratocast™ is designed to meet the security demands of small and midsized businesses, which require a reliable video surveillance solution without the associated costs of on-premises storage, software updates and on-going maintenance.

ISC West 2016

VIVOTEK showcased the latest technology in network video surveillance at ISC WEST. The solutions included ultimate bandwidth and storage saving solutions, retail and discreet form factors, the improved zoom bullet camera and the collaboration with Genetec™ ’s Stratocast Cloud Platform, as well as PoE solutions.

Secure Mexico's INNOVASPORT Stores

To upgrade the current security and surveillance systems, Novalan Computadoras y Redes installed 1044 VIVOTEK’s network cameras in 69 INNOVASPORT chain stores in Mexico. And this has further enhanced the efficiency of store security and expense saving in security guards.
