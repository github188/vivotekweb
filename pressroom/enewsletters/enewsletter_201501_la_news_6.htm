Hi Power 4 ports PoE Switch

Max 52W Per Port

The solution for installation is also key for surveillance systems. VIVOTEK proudly announces our Hi Power 4 Ports PoE Switch, solving the problem of single port power budget (30W) limited issue from other non-surveillance purpose designed PoE Switch. VIVOTEK Hi Power PoE Switch can provide a Maximum 52 watts per port, and you can manually switch off the port that does not need to provide power. The rest of the power budget can be used by other PoE devices that are connected to this PoE Switch.

Switch vs Injector

Compared with other PoE Injectors, which can provide such high power over Ethernet, the maximum distance from Injector to Switch or the Server is 1M. VIVOTEK Hi Power PoE Switch can easily go to 100M to camera side or to the switch and server side. You can now choose the total power budget, 65 Watts or 120 watts to fit your surveillance project.

High power PoE Switch with extender solving problems without local power

This AW-FET-050A-065 or AW-FET-050A-120 can be used with our World’s first outdoor IP camera-R Series with embedded PoE repeater. For dome type cameras, VIVOTEK also designed the Conduit box with 2 Port PoE Extender to make your installation more streamlined, saving on your installation cost and time, also making it easier to maintain. High Power PoE Switch can also work in an environment without local power. Foror example, using one high power PoE switch (AW-FED-0500-120) with three-two port extenders (AP-FXC-0200), can power seven cameras without any local power.

This VIVOTEK Hi Power PoE switch is definitely the solution you must introduce to your customers to provide a reliable and revolutionary installation design.
