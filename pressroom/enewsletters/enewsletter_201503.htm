VIVOTEK Upgrades Its Central Management Software VAST to Version 1.10 and Its Portable Software Tool Shepherd

VIVOTEK will release VAST Version 1.10. In this version, VIVOTEK introduces three advanced features to VAST users, specifically, Logical Tree, Standard VCA Event Integration, and Object Counting Report. Additionally, in order to facilitate the configuration and setting of VIVOTEK network, cameras, VIVOTEK enhanced its portable software tool Shepherd and equipped it with more powerful functions.

 

VIVOTEK Is to Launch Two New PoE Switches

VIVOTEK will launch two new PoE switches in April, one is unmanaged 16-port PoE Switch AW-FGT-180A-250 and one is 8-port VivoCam L2 Managed PoE Switch AW-GEV-104A-130. The unmanaged 16-port PoE Switch AW-FGT-180A-250, more advanced than previous version, is especially designed with 2 combo Gigabit Ethernet (RJ45/SFP), facilitating the direct connection with NVR and devices at control center and making the data transmission easier and faster. Following the launch of 24-port managed L2 PoE Gigabit switch in Jan, VIVOTEK designed the 8-port version for meeting clients' diverse need.
