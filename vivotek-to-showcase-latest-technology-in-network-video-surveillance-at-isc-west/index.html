VIVOTEK to Showcase Latest Technology in Network Video Surveillance at ISC WEST

2015.04.14

San Jose, Calif. – April 15, 2015 – VIVOTEK USA, Inc. a leading manufacturer in the network video surveillance industry, will be featuring a wide range of new products throughout the largest security event in North America, ISC WEST (Booth #8073) at Sands Expo & Convention Center in Las Vegas, Nevada on April 15th through 17th. ISC WEST security tradeshow offers the perfect opportunity to meet with over 28,000 security professionals in the industry allowing attendees to keep up with the latest and greatest technology as well as market trends.

VIVOTEK's booth will contain demos of its latest technology, including PoE solution IB8367-R, panoramic solutions CC8370-HV and FE8182, AW-GEV-264A-185 Vivocam PoE switch and ND8322P NVR. VIVOTEK's bullet camera IB8367-R, is the first outdoor network camera designed with built-in PoE extender, allowing multiple cameras to be daisy chained, sharing one single power source and extending PoE distance up to 300 meters. The PoE extender enables long-distance surveillance system administration, in various outdoor applications such as factory areas, parking lots, crossroads and outdoor sidewalks. Equipped with Smart Stream and 3DNR technology, as well as removable IR-cut filter with built-in IR illuminators, viewing is clear up to 30 meters even in the most challenging lighting conditions. VIVOTEK will also demonstrate their complete panoramic solution lineup including CC8370-HV and FE8182 providing the upmost discretion and complete coverage in challenging conditions. CC8370-HV 3-Megapixel CMOS sensor, wide dynamic range vandal proof camera comes equipped with MicroSD/SDHC/SDXC card slot as well as built in microphone and supports local dewarping. Encased in an IP66/IK-10 vandal proof housing and adjustable lens angle at ±25°, CC8370-HV is ideal for retail applications. FE8182 5-Megapixel CMOS sensor panoramic fisheye will also be on display at ISC West, providing 180° viewing by wall mount and 360°viewing by ceiling mount. FE8182 supports local dewarping with built in microphone as well as MicroSD/SDHC/SDXC card slot for back up storage.

Included in VIVOTEK's complete and total solution is AW-GEV-264A-185, a 24-port managed L2 PoE Gigabit Switch with unique IP Surveillance Management Functions. Otherwise known as VivoCam PoE switch, this piece of equipment makes it easy to manage, configure and monitor VIVOTEK IP cameras, video servers, NVRs and central management software. VIVOTEK will also present ND8322P, Embedded Plug & Play NVR. The Linux embedded ND8322P is an 8 channel standalone NVR with 8-port 802.3at/af complaint PoE, allowing connection with PoE cameras for a maximum 80 W power supply. ND8322P comes supplied with "one button setup" which allows plug-&-play as well as auto-setup functionality making the apparatus simple to install.

VIVOTEK invites you to visit their booth #8073 at ISC West on April 15th through 17th in Las Vegas, Nevada at Sands Expo & Convention Center. Explore firsthand the advancements VIVOTEK has made in the HD and megapixel IP surveillance industry by providing total security solutions.
