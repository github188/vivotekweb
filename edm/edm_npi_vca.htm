Line Crossing Detection, Field Detection, Object Counting
   
 

Dear VIVOTEKer,

We are pleased to introduce our first video content analysis (VCA) application package, which includes three key functions: Field Detection, Line Crossing Detection, and Object Counting. Introduction of the new VCA application package follows the debut of the VIVOTEK Application Development Platform (VADP), an open platform for integrating video analytic functionality within its cameras. Now, the VCA package can analyze data directly on a camera to provide solutions for business applications, in addition to traditional security use cases.

The full VIVOTEK video content analysis suite, including Field Detection, Line Crossing Detection, and Object Counting modules, is available immediately on VIVOTEK’s MD8562, FE8174 / 74V, and FE8173 cameras. The best news for customer is they are free to get license in these models. We plan to integrate Field Detection and Line Crossing functions in other camera models in the near future.
