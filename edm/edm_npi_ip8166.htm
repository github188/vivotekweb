Dear VIVOTEKer,

For empowering day/night city surveillance applications, VIVOTEK is pleased to announce the Fixed Network Camera, IP8166. The Full HD professional box network camera is armed with SNV and Smart Stream II technologies, not only capturing high-definition images in low light environments but able to optimize quality for desired regions and thus ensure maximum efficiency of bandwidth usage. The IP8166 can reduce both bandwidth and storage consumption by up to 50% while retaining the same superb image quality as a Full HD camera.

To meet the demands of diverse scenarios, the IP8166 is compatible with a variety of accessories, from lens, external IR LEDs, enclosures and PoE solutions. With the value-added support and flexibility of an abundance of professional accessories, the IP8166 is perfect, ideal for traffic application, long-range application, wide temperature application or cabinet application with single to multiple camera surveillance.
