CC8371-HV

 
 

180° Panoramic IR Camera

 
   
 

3MP • 30 fps • 15M IR • 180° HFOV • SNV • WDR Pro • Smart Stream II

   
 

Dear VIVOTEKer,

VIVOTEK’s CC8371-HV is a vandal-proof IR fisheye network camera offering up to 30 fps at 3-Megapixel resolution. Featuring 180° horizontal panoramic views with adjustable 25° tilt angle, the camera allows users amazing flexibility of viewing angles and options. The camera’s weather-proof compact size also makes it an ideal choice for indoor and outdoor scenarios such as boutiques, convenience stores, banks, schools, parking lots and residential homes.

The CC8371-HV's biggest improvement is added IR illumination. With these specially designed built-in IR illuminators, the CC8371-HV is now capable of viewing 180° panoramic night time video up to 15 meters. So no matter day or night, light or dark, the CC8371-HV's versatility is unmatched.

Finally, the CC8371-HV's additional features such as WDR Pro, Smart Stream II, and SNV Technology enable amazing panoramic video at all hours of the day. Even further increasing its abilities, a built-in microphone is included to support recording sound within a 5-meter radius.
