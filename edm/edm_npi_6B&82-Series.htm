For an ever-expanding range of surveillance deployments, we are pleased to announce VIVOTEK’s new versatile V-Pro network cameras, the 6B series and 82 series with premium features for 24/7 surveillance. The 6B series features Full HD images at 30 fps, with SNV and WDR technology. The 82 provides 5 megapixel images at 15 fps, with WDR Enhanced and Smart Stream technology. Both series are not only equipped with multiple focusing methods and wide operating temperature range options, but highly flexible and expandable to meet a wide range of requirements.
