AW-GEV Series

 
 

VivoCam Layer 2 Managed PoE Switches

 
   
 

Managing IP Surveillance • Managed L2 Gigabit PoE Switch

   
 

Dear VIVOTEKer,

We are pleased to introduce the world’s first Layer 2 Managed PoE Switch with IP surveillance management functions, the AW-GEV series, enabling to seamlessly manage, configure, and monitor all types of VIVOTEK devices, such as network cameras, video servers, NVRs and CMS. This series includes three models, including the AW-GEV-104A-130 with 8xGE PoE + 2xGE UTP/SF ports and AW-GEV-264A-185/ AW-GEV-264A-370 with 24xGE PoE + 2xCombo GE ports.

The AW-GEV series also features Graphic Monitoring, such as Topology view, Floor view and Google Map view for controlling and understanding network structure and device situation. It not only features surveillance network environment set up functions, but troubleshooting and traffic monitoring functions also help users to clarify and troubleshoot any connection or device issue. In addition, users can also back up their IP camera configuration files, and batch re-boot/re-store camera device through the VivoCam PoE switch. With all of these features and more, the AW-GEV series is the best PoE solution to install and manage your VIVOTEK devices.
