CC8370-HV

 
 

Anti-Ligature Fisheye Network Camera

 
   
 

3MP, WDR Pro, 3DNR, 180° Panoramic View, IK10, IP66, SNV, Anti-Ligature, Anti-Grip

   
 

Dear VIVOTEKer,

We are pleased to announce the anti-ligature fisheye network camera, the CC8370-HV. This compact 3-Megapixel fisheye network camera features an anti-grip design, vandal resistant housing and 180 degree panoramic views which simultaneously ensures the safety of customers, staffs, patients or detainees, making it ideal for high security environments, such as banks, schools, retails, detention houses, holding cells, psychiatric wards, and correctional institutions.

Featuring 180 degree horizontal panoramic views with an adjustable 25 degree tilt angle, the CC8370-HV allows security users to see any event with zero blind spots. Thanks to a built-in microphone, sound within a 5 meter radius can also be clearly recorded.

The CC8370-HV is also armed with a robust IP66-rated and IK10-rated housing to withstand water and dust and to protect against vandalism or tampering. To ensure the 24/7 surveillance, it equipped with WDR Pro, which captures both the dark and bright areas of an image and combines the differences to create a highly realistic representation of the original scene. Further, with the SNV (Supreme Night Visibility) technology, it is empowered to provide high quality images under low-light conditions. This superior visibility at night time further enables the CC8370-HV to eliminate unexpected and unwanted incidents.

By integrating with VIVOTEK’s free video management software, ST7501, users can set up IP surveillance systems with ease and plan ahead by setting up numerous alarm triggers, such as motion detection and camera tampering detection. This seamless surveillance of VIVOTEK IP solutions not only results in superb image quality but also distribute the effective management without any delay.
