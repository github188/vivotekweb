FE8191 & FE8391-V

 
 

Ultra-Megapixel
Fisheye Network Camera

 
   
 

12MP COMS Sensor, 360° Surround View, 10 Meter IR, Smart IR, 3DNR, IP66, IK10, Pixel Calculator, PPTZ

   
 

Dear VIVOTEKer,

We are pleased to announce the Market-Leading Ultra-Megapixel fisheye network cameras, the FE8191 and the FE8391-V. The FE8391-V uniquely features a 12MP CMOS sensor and built-in IR LEDs, making this model the world's first Ultra-Megapixel fisheye with IR illumination, consequently satisfying consumers' demand for products with diversified applications.

The FE8191 and FE8391-V are both designed with 12MP CMOS sensors and 1.5 mm lens, providing guarantee excellent image quality and 180° panoramic (wall mount) or 360° surround viewing (ceiling/floor mount). And both cameras are compatible with the VIVOTEK Panoramic PTZ solution, which allow users to simultaneously monitor an area overview from a fisheye model while providing the capability for a detailed regional view from a speed dome, and 3D Noise Reduction technology which enable the cameras to capture clear, polished video under low-light conditions.

In addition, the FE8191 and FE8391-V are designed with a removable IR-cut filter and WDR Enhancement technology, which allows the cameras to maintain optimal image quality around the clock and unparalleled visibility under highly contrasted lighting environments.

Moreover, the FE8391-V is designed with a vandal-proof IK10-rated and IP66-rated weatherproof housing, enhancing the camera's durability against harsh environmental conditions and vandalism. The design of built-in IR LEDs and patent-protected light cup enables the FE8391-V to provide uniformly and even 360° surround illumination for clear viewing up to distances of 10 meters, even in completely dark environments.
