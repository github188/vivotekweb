Fixed Network Camera

Dear VIVOTEKer,

We are pleased to introduce an exciting new box camera, IP816A-HP, offering up to 60 fps @ 2-Megapixel resolution with superb image quality. The IP816A-HP features WDR Pro (100dB) and SNV to 0.03 Lux making it suitable for environments with challenging lighting conditions, such as parking lot entrances, license plate recognition and traffic monitoring on high-ways.

What's more, IP816A-HP has also added additional functionality for specific applications, such as RBF (remote back focus) support with focus assist, video rotation, EIS (electronic image stability), snapshot focus and Lens profile design.
