MS8392-EV

 

12MP Multiple Sensor Network Camera

   
 

12MP • 180° Panoramic View • IP66 • IK10 • -50°C ~ 50°C

   
 

Dear VIVOTEKer,

We are pleased to release the multi-sensor dome camera, MS8392-EV. Featuring 4 x 3MP CMOS sensors stitched together, the MS8392-EV can achieve a seamless 12MP 180° panoramic image, dramatically reducing the total number of cameras needed for surveillance and thereby helping to reduce total installation time and costs. Additionally, the MS8392-EV is armed with a robust IP66 and IK10-rated housing and wide operating temperature range from -50°C to 50°C, making it ideally suitable for large, open areas such as parking lots, shopping malls, schools and parks & plazas.
