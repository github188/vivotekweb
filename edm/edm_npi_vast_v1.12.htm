We are pleased to release the upgrade of VIVOTEK’s VAST, Version 1.12. To enhance the flexibility of IP surveillance management, this version provides multi-stream recording function to help users set different streams for different purposes, which will efficiently save users’ bandwidth and storage costs.

This not only improves intuitive and practical functions for users, but also makes great advancements in the VCA Counting Reports. By implementing VIVOTEK’s people counting cameras in retail applications, VAST can provide multi-spot integration analytics, scheduled export reports by email, and also auto-sync the database in the camera to VAST after recovering from network connection failures, thereby securing seamless counting data all the time. The VCA Counting Reports will enrich VIVOTEK’s People Counting Solution in retail applications, allowing business owners to optimize their operating decisions.

New Features:
