Dear VIVOTEKer,

We are pleased to introduce the advanced speed dome network camera, SD8364E, offering extra smooth video quality with resolution up to 30 fps @1080p and 30x optical zoom lens to capture details at top-notch quality. In addition to keep the cutting edge technology of speed dome series, such as auto tracking, audio detection, WDR Pro and over tilt angle up to 220 degrees, the SD8364E further provides EIS (electronic image stabilizer) and video defog function to maintain clear images. Moreover, the SD8364E also supports VIVOTEK unique Panoramic PTZ, combining the fisheye camera and the speed dome to make a perfect panoramic solution. With all features, the SD8364E is the best long range speed dome camera in the market.
