IP816A-LPC Kit

 
 

License Plate Capture Solution

 
   
 

2MP • SNV • 3DNR • Remote Back Focus • EIS • P-iris • 60 fps • Snapshot Focus • Corridor View

   
 

Dear VIVOTEKer,

We are pleased to announce our first dedicated license plate capture solution, the IP816A-LPC Kit. This professional 2-Megapixel Solution Kit with external IR LEDs is specially designed for 24/7 day and night traffic monitoring environments such as streets, tunnels and parking lots.

The IP816A-LPC Kit is designed to capture vehicles travelling at speeds of up to 60MPH (90 km/h). With 2-Megapixel resolution, the IP816A-LPC Kit is able to provide wide coverage views of dual-lane traffic, ideally offering scene overview and LPC view for recognition. Featured with Headlight filter design, it can avoid overexposure and glare effect, and capture detailed information from the plates in different light conditions. Moreover, value-added features such as EIS, RBF, Snapshot Focus, Vandal-Proof IK10-rated and Weather-Proof IP66-rated Housing, all make the IP816A-LPC Kit perfect for LPR solutions.

Meanwhile, the IP816A-LPC Kit is also tested with the Overseer LPR software developed by VIT Company, one of the famous LPR software manufacturers in the world. This synergy further empowers the IP816A-LPC Kit to capture license plate images for any LPR application.
