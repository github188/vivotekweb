FD8166A-N

 

Ultra-mini IR Fixed Dome Camera

   
 

2MP • 30 fps • 940nm IR • Stylish Design • SNV • WDR Enhanced • Smart Stream II • Easy Installation

   
 

Dear VIVOTEKer,

We are pleased to announce the ultra-mini IR Fixed Dome Network Camera, FD8166A-N. By leveraging the compact design of FD8166A (only 90mm in diameter), great image quality and affordable price, this Full HD camera is ideal for indoor surveillance such as retail, offices, banks, and other public areas.

Equipped with WDR Enhanced and SNV technology, the FD8166A-N is capable of capturing high-definition images in both high contrast and low light environments. Armed with invisible 940nm IR illumination, the camera is able to see up to 6 meters in total darkness. When combined with VIVOTEK’s Smart Stream II technology, it is able to optimize image quality for desired regions and thus ensure maximum efficiency of bandwidth usage. The FD8166A-N can reduce both bandwidth and storage consumption by up to 50% while retaining superb image quality. As an all-in-one camera, the FD8166A-N is designed for easy installation and PoE powered, making setup both quick and simple.
