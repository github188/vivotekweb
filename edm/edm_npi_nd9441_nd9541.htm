Dear VIVOTEKer,

Embracing VIVOTEK’s new H.265/HEVC surveillance solutions, we are pleased to release the new generation of H.265 compatible NVRs, the 16-CH ND9441, and 32-CH ND9541. Both NVRs fully support H.265 video compression technology and up to 24TB storage capacity with 4HDD, which offer 30% to 50% more recording capacity than other similar systems equipped only for H.264 compression.

Meanwhile, fisheye de-warping function enables a panoramic image to be recorded while areas of interest are displayed in precise detailed images. Further, both NVRs equip with dual Ethernet ports, providing the flexibility to cope with a network failover situation or multi-IP setting. Thanks to the EZConnect APP, users are able to remotely monitor their surveillance system from any iOS or Android device at any time, from anywhere. Both NVRs are fully integrated with VAST, VIVOTEK’s free central management software, which is developed to provide a broad range of advanced event management functions. With VAST, users can manage multiple H.265 NVRs and set up their IP surveillance system quickly and intuitively.
