Course Description

VIVOTEK Product Expert (VPE) Training is a product focused course covering VIVOTEK’s comprehensive product line with emphasis on camera technology and setup. In this 6-hour course (9am-4pm), attendees will learn about VIVOTEK products and features through a series of hands-on labs, demonstrations, and discussions. In addition, we will introduce mobile device application tools that are useful in selecting the right camera for specific applications and demonstrate remote camera monitoring software using an iOS and Android handheld device.

What to expect

  • Product line introduction and discussion
  • VIVOTEK advanced features
  • Mobile application demonstration:
    • VIVOTEK iViewer
    • VIVOTEK iVolume
    • VIVOTEK Design Tool
  • Attendees will have access to a VIVOTEK camera and learn to configure and setup:
    • Motion detection
    • Network settings
    • Events and triggers
    • Image settings
    • Edge recording (SD)
    • (e)PTZ preset locations
    • Digital I/O
    • Camera management
    • Video streams

Requirements

  • Attendees are required to bring a laptop for the hands-on labs. It is encouraged to review the VIVOTEK IP Surveillance Handbook prior to attending the class.
  • Windows Home / Professional Laptop with Internet Explorer Required!

Cost
