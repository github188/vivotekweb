VIVOTEK USA receives Best Supplier Delivery award

2015.02.25

San Jose, Calif. - VIVOTEK today announced that it has won a 2014 Vendor Partner Award from ADI during the Vendor Symposium at ADI’s annual 2015 Kickoff Meeting in ChampionsGate, Fla.

VIVOTEK is proud to be among those recognized, and honored to receive the 3rd place award in the Best Supplier Delivery (OTTR-on time to request) performance category for North America.

ADI, a leading global wholesale distributor of security and low voltage products with more than 95,000 products from more than 550 vendor partners, recognizes major suppliers across the United States, Canada and Puerto Rico for their continued contributions and assistance toward the success of the company. Vendor performance was assessed by ADI’s sales and leadership teams and contingent upon revenue, sales support, marketing initiatives and inventory results in the categories of Vendor of the Year, New Product of the Year, Best Sales Support, Best Marketing Support, Rookie Vendor of the Year and Best Supplier Delivery Performance.

"We were recognized for having the 3rd Best Supplier Delivery (OTTR) performance for 2014 out of all the vendors ADI carries. As this is something of immense importance to ADI, it’s great to see VIVOTEK recognized. This is a prestigious award and something we are extremely proud to promote," said National Sales Manager of East Coast Division Alan Green.

"We are proud to congratulate all the ADI 2014 Vendor Award honorees," said Michael Flink, President of ADI Global. "We appreciate VIVOTEK’s support and dedication to deliver quality products and provide excellent service to our dealer base."

VIVOTEK USA is proud of its accomplishments working alongside ADI in a very competitive industry. It strives to ensure projects are done correctly and in a timely manner with customer satisfaction as top priority. VIVOTEK USA looks forward to providing continued support to ADI throughout 2015.
