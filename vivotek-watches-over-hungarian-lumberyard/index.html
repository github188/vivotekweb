VIVOTEK Watches Over Hungarian Lumberyard

A lumberyard in Nyirgelsei, Hungary, once relied on security guards for factory security, but the large share of spending on staffing had driven the manager to seek more efficient security solutions at a lower cost. Besides, the manager was hoping to expand monitoring sites inside and outside the factory to make sure all processes in compliance with regulations. Thus, VIVOTEK's Hungarian distributor Kelly-Tech Kft helped the lumberyard replace the old-fashioned security approach with an IP surveillance system using VIVOTEK's network cameras.

Due to the small area of each chain store, the easy-to-use IP7133 and PT7135 network cameras were selected. The PT7135s were installed to offer an overview of the entire store with their pan/tilt capability while the IP7133s were aimed to monitor customers in key spots including the counter, the commodity shelves and the entrance. Both of the IP7133s and PT7135s come with a compact, stylish design that blends into the decor so customers do not feel intimidated during shopping. Also, the cameras provide 3G support, allowing the manager to keep an eye on any of the stores without traveling to and fro.

VIVOTKE's IP7135, PZ6122 and SD7151 network cameras were called upon for this project. The PZ6122s with wireless connection were installed inside the factory because the manager wanted to avoid problems of running and maintaining IP network cables in the working area. With pan/tilt and 10x zoom functions that provide wide field of view with substantial details, the PZ6122s can significantly improve surveillance performance and efficiency. Inside the offices, the easy-to-use IP7135s were installed. Both of the PZ6122s and IP7135 boast outstanding low light performance, making them perfect for indoor environments.

For outdoor surveillance including the yard and the entrance corridor, the SD7151 speed dome cameras were installed which come with a vandal- and weather-proof housing for protection against rain and dust. Because the SD7151s feature 360°continuous rotation as well as 18x zoom capability, the manager can monitor any spot of interest with crisp details. Since the low light level at night will affect camera image quality, the SD7151's built-in IR-cut filter can guarantee constantly clear images day and night.

VIVOTEK's IP surveillance system offers an efficient and centralized security solution through which the manager can monitor the lumberyard remotely anytime and anywhere. "The network cameras enable us to maintain lumberyard security at a lower cost compared to using a CCTV system because we only need to connect them to our IP networks and PC systems. The best part is we have full control over the lumberyard wherever we are." said the manager of the lumberyard.
